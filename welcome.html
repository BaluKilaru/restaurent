Welcome To Balu Spices

We Are The Best

Indian cuisine is a natural part of the diet in the markets in which the Balu Spices operate. We continue to offer new menu options, both vegetarian and non-vegetarian, largely based on Indian cuisine and use seasonal customer preferences to introduce new dishes. We also run popular food festivals at our Balu Spices, thereby offering our customers a range of Indian and international cuisines.

Our fixed price ‘all you can eat’ concept at our Balu Spices offers our customers a varying spread of consistently delicious food at a value-oriented price in a pleasant and casual dining environment. We believe that Balu Spices cuisine is distinctively attractive as compared with other dining options due to its DIY and cooked-at-the-table nature and the flexibility and fun of mixing and matching a wide variety of meats, vegetables, sauces and condiments to create dishes according to each diner’s individual tastes and preferences. In addition, fixed price dining is very popular amongst relatively larger groups, due to customers’ perception of value for money and the comfort of certainty over the bill amount irrespective of varying individual appetites and consumption
